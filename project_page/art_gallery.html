What I did & What I learned

Throughout my work on the SunWATER+ project, I gained invaluable experience in designing user-centric interfaces for complex systems. Collaborating with interdisciplinary teams of engineers and researchers taught me how to balance technical requirements with intuitive design. Additionally, I deepened my understanding of sustainable energy solutions, particularly solar-powered greywater reuse, and learned how to integrate technologies like AI and nanotechnology into scalable systems. This project enhanced my ability to approach design challenges holistically, ensuring the user interface supports both functionality and ease of use.
