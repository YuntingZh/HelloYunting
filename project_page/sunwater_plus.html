SunWATER+ is an innovative water management system integrating AI-powered monitoring and solar-based greywater reuse technology. Design Context

Two main questions were asked at the beginning design stage:

1. Who is our user?

Building managers and households

2. What are some challenges they are facing?

Building managers need efficient tools to monitor multiple units in real-time, while households require clear and actionable data about their water usage. Additionally, balancing energy savings with effective greywater reuse can be complex.

Color-Coded Water Status
Using a three-color system (green, yellow, red) to represent water quality, making it easy for users to assess the system's health at a glance.

Pop-Up Filter Notifications
Designed pop-up notifications for mobile users to alert them when the filter needs to be changed, ensuring system maintenance isn‚Äôt overlooked.

Lock Screen Wedge
Included a small wedge on the lock screen to display the water status, allowing users to access critical information without unlocking their phones.

Real-Time Visual Feedback
Provided dynamic color changes on the dashboard to reflect real-time water quality, enabling users to react quickly to system changes.

Responsive and Scalable Design
Ensured the UI is responsive across devices, offering a seamless experience for both building managers and individual users on any platform.

Design Highlights

To facilitate water management tasks, we designed a system that displays real-time data for building managers and households through a user-friendly dashboard. The dashboard provides crucial insights into water usage, filtration status, and energy efficiency. Additionally, the AI-powered assistant in the system offers maintenance alerts and optimization suggestions for water-saving practices.

Design Thinking Example 2

Simplifying Water and Energy Data Visualization

SunWATER+ displays complex water and energy data through simplified visuals, making it easier for users to track metrics. We implemented both 2D dashboards for key metrics (e.g., water usage, energy savings) and 3D visualizations for building managers to see a high-level view of system performance across multiple buildings.

2D Dashboard UI

3D Performance Visualization

We optimized user experience by simplifying real-time data, ensuring that users could focus on the most important metrics first. A brief overview provides a scan of the current water quality, while detailed graphs and metrics allow users to dive deeper into the specifics.

Design Thinking Example 3

Use visual feedback to assist decision-making

SunWATER+ uses visual and audio feedback to confirm when actions are taken or when the system needs attention. For example, if a water filtration issue arises, the system highlights the relevant part of the dashboard, while also sending notifications. This ensures that building managers and households can quickly resolve issues without needing to continuously monitor the system.
