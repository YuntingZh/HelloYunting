Puzzle Now - Apple Vision Pro Puzzle Game

This project was an extension of a hackathon, where I worked in a team of three to create a fun puzzle game for the Apple Vision Pro upon its release. 'Puzzle Now' offers an immersive puzzle experience, allowing users to interact with puzzle pieces in a spatial environment. With varying difficulty levels and automatic clustering of pieces, the game provides both challenge and entertainment, enhanced by interactive sound and feedback. Now available on the Apple Store.

Key Technologies: Apple Vision Pro, SwiftUI, Spatial Interaction

Role: Game Designer

Mix Reality - Boba Making Experience

During an exciting hackathon at Stanford, We build a mixed reality experience focused on making boba tea. Using Unity and the Quest 3, we allowed users to create their digital twin of a boba cup, with the recipe adjusting based on the cup's volume. This project brought together interactivity, creativity, and real-time adjustments.

Key Technologies: Unity, Quest 3, Mixed Reality

Role: Developer and Designer

Mr. Fossil

"Mr. Fossil" is an MR-based educational experience designed to teach children about paleontology in an engaging and interactive way. Utilizing MR technology, users can place and explore 3D fossil models in their real-world environment, learn fun facts, and even interact with animated dinosaurs. The app encourages a hands-on approach to learning, making the experience both educational and entertaining. This project showcases my skills in creating interactive MR content with a focus on education and user engagement.

Key Technologies: Unity, 3D Modeling, Animation

XR Ophthalmoscopy Simulator - AR + VR Solution for Education

The XR Ophthalmoscopy Simulator is part of a research collaboration with the IDSC and Bascom Palmer Eye Institute. This VR tool is designed to help medical students practice eye examinations, offering an immersive, risk-free learning environment with realistic 3D eye models and varied scenarios to enhance diagnostic skills.

Key Technologies: AR, VR, 3D Modeling, Medical Simulation

Role: XR Developer, Medical Simulation Designer
